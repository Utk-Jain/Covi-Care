COVID-19 is an infectious disease caused by novel coronavirus.

Human coronaviruses are common and are typically associated with mild to moderate respiratory illnesses, similar to the common cold.
It is now recognized as Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome Coronavirus-2 (SARS-CoV-2; formerly called 2019-nCoV).

What are the Symptoms of COVID-19 ?

SYMPTOMS of Corona Virus

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.Symptoms may take up to 14 days to appear after exposure to COVID-19. This is the longest known incubation period for this disease.

Fever

Fever is a key symptom, experts say. Don't fixate on a number, but know it's really not a fever until temperature reaches at least 100 degrees.

Cough

Coughing is another key symptom, but it's not just any cough. It should be a dry cough that you feel in your chest and it causes pain in your throat.

Difficulty Breathing

Shortness of breath can be a third and very serious manifestation of Covid-19, and it can occur on its own, without a cough and causing a faster breathing.

Loss of Taste or Smell

COVID-19 typically produces a range of flu-like symptoms, including a cough and fatigue, but it can also cause the loss of taste and smell. Taste and smell can return or get better within 4 weeks of the virus clearing the body.

Diarrhea

People with COVID-19 may experience gastrointestinal symptoms like diarrhea, vomiting, or loss of appetite. These symptoms might occur alone or with other flu-like symptoms such as fever and coughing.

HOW DOES IT SPREAD ?

Coronaviruses are most commonly spread from an infected person through or also through surfaces where the virus is residing. Some ways through which the virus gets spread are :

  • Respiratory droplets when you cough or sneeze. They get into the air and gets into the body when someone breathes in.
  • Close personal contact, such as touching, shaking hands or touching some unsanitised surfaces and objects.
  • Touching something with the virus on it, then touching your eyes, nose or mouth before washing your hands and letting the virus to get in.

These viruses are not known to spread through ventilation systems or through water. Mainly they spread through physical contacts and touches.

How do we prevent Covid-19?

PREVENTION OF CORONAVIRUS

Prevention is always better than cure. The best way to prevent the spread of corona virus and other infection is to :

Wash your hands

Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds to remove infectious germs from your hands.

Avoid Touching

Avoid touching your eyes, nose or mouth, especially with unwashed hands and don't touch unnecessary things.

Avoid Close Contact

Avoid close contact with people who are sick and also avoid going into gatherings.

Cover Your Mouth

Cover your mouth and nose with your arm or tissues and use face masks to reduce and prevent the spread of the virus and other germs.

Use Dustbin

Immediately dispose of any tissues and masks you have used into the garbage as soon as possible and wash your hands afterwards.

Clean and Disinfect

Clean and disinfect frequently touched objects and surfaces, such as electronic devices, doorknobs and maintain good hygiene.

Stay Home

Stay in your home if you are sick or have any symptoms in order to avoid spreading illness to others.

If you have SYMPTOMS of COVID-19 — fever, cough, difficulty breathing etc, Then :

Stay home

Stay home to avoid spreading it to others — if you live with others, stay in a separate room or keep a 2-metre distance.
